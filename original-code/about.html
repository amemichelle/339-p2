About Us

Are you looking for a taste of the West Coast?

Founded in June 2020, Cafe California is a new chain of cafes throughout the United States and Canada.

With a number of strong coffees, piping-hot teas, sea inspired sweets, and merchandise made by local artisans, we strive to bring the warmth of the California coast with each product we sell, whether online or in store.

As a company, we strive to impact our community in a positive way through radical transparency. We strive to:

  1. Work with local artisans to create our merchandise for our stores, to be sold online and in-store.
  2. Source our ingredient sustainability and responsibly by utilizing local supply chains.
  3. Donate 10% of our profits to Oceana, the world’s largest organization dedicated to marine conservation.

We hope you’ll help us carry out our mission by shopping at our online store, or stopping by one of our locations!
