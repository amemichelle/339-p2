FAQ's

It's been x amount of time and my order hasn't shown up! What should I do?

Unfortunately once a package has been shipped, we do not have much control of the parcel and its wearabouts. Be sure to contact your local post office in regards to potential lost packages. In addition, feel free to contact us and we will do our best to assist you.

What is your return policy?

All online orders can be returned or exchanged at one of our many locations. Contact one of our customer service representatives to discuss other online options that may be a better fit for you. All returns must occur 30 days after receiving a product.

How can I make a bulk purchase?

Bulk purchases can be arranged for pickup at one of our many locations. Please allow at least 72 hours notice before your deadline. We at Cafe California would love to help your next event be a success!
