Product Description

Our Brazillian split blend is the base for our light-bodied beach ball blend. The blend has notes of red fruit with a floral undertone, which is sure to get you excited for the summertime. The best time to try out this blend is during the summer.

All blends bought online come whole. In store, we can pre-grind upon request.
